New release

New Single “Army Dreamers”

At last we will be releasing our viral version of “Army Dreamers”, the Kate Bush classic as arranged by Ash McGlynn, to all streaming services on April 24th. Recorded in Dublin by Brian Masterson, this song means a huge amount to us, reflecting the times we live in alongside the revival in interest in Kate Bush, who was a formative influence on so many of us.

You can see the revamped video of “Army Dreamers” alongside this announcement.

You can pre-save the single on Apple Music. and Spotify here.

Update

SYSTIR Go Viral & 2026

We have to apologise for not updating our site for ages. We have been so busy mixing our debut album, which is due very soon.

We have gone a bit mad on a few platforms, with our cover of the Kate Bush song “Army Dreamers” getting millions of views on Instagram and TikTok. We have loads of plans for this year: our UK debut in May, our return to Germany in October, and Oulu, Finland in November, plus some very interesting dates coming up in Latvia and Estonia.

Touring

New Dates: China, Iceland & Germany

We are really pleased to let you all know that we will be very busy in the next while, with an extensive debut tour in China this October to organise. The tour will take us across the country over two weeks.

Before that, we have concerts coming up in Iceland in May, our first time there, alongside some very special appearances in various places. You can find details on the Live page, including our debut in Germany at Mainzer Musiksommer in late July.

Also to come are more appearances, so follow us for details. Caitríona beautifully captures the classic “Skye Boat Song” in her own inimitable way.

Film premiere

ECHOES OF IRELAND Premiere Sunday February 9th

This Sunday at 6pm GMT on YouTube, 1pm EST, join us to view Michael McGlynn’s biographical film “Echoes of Ireland - The Music of Michael McGlynn”, which features SYSTIR alongside a host of extraordinary musicians, composers, and academics.

The film is currently free to watch on YouTube.

Performance

SYSTIR and Game of Thrones

Last weekend we were lucky enough to spend time in The Kicking Donkey & George’s Bar in Bundoran singing a few of the fine songs from Game of Thrones. The beautiful “Jenny of Oldstones” has a solo sung by Lorna, and “The Rains of Castamere” is probably the best-known song from the series.

You can hear these and a selection of other songs from Game of Thrones on the 2nd of December. To celebrate, we have a special giveaway for fans of ANÚNA, SYSTIR, and M’ANAM. Enter the discount code Systir20 for 20% off.

We hope to see you there.
